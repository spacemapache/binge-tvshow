Brooklyn Nine-Nine

About Brooklyn Nine-Nine

Comedy series following the exploits of Det. Jake Peralta and his diverse, lovable colleagues as they police the NYPD's 99th Precinct.
Source: IMDB

Why should you watch Brooklyn Nine-Nine?

It's a light hearted show, therefore making it an easy binge worthy show.
The pilot is engaging which had me hooked right from the beginning and it has become my comfort show. I love the jokes and the character developments.

Favourite characters

Rosa Diaz

Tough and intimidating, however Rosa has a heart of gold.

Captain Holt

Lots of deadpan expressions and hilarious dry jokes.

Jake Peralta

All around goofball but great character development.
